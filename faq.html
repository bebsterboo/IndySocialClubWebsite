Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Everything you've ever wondered about The Indy Social Club, in one place.

What is The Indy Social Club?

The Indy Social Club is a free community for young professionals in the Indianapolis area who want to meet new people and try new things. We host social events all over the city to make it easy to connect, explore, and build real friendships. In practice, we are a hub for people and groups looking to meet others in the Indianapolis area. An Indy Social Club host organizes many of the events, but many events are held by others as well.

Is it really free to join?

Yes! There's no membership fee or sign-up cost to be part of the community or RSVP to most events. Some events (like ticketed nights out) do have a cost, but those are clearly marked.

Who is this community for?

Anyone in the Indianapolis area who's looking to make new friends! We tend to skew toward young professionals (20s and 30s), but everyone is welcome. Whether you just moved to Indy, are coming out of a transition in your life, or simply want to expand your social circle, this is for you.

How do I RSVP for an event?

Head to our Events page or check the Calendar to see what's coming up. Click the RSVP button on any event you're interested in and follow the steps from there.

What if I don't know anyone? Will I feel awkward?

Honestly? Most people who show up come solo. That's the whole point. Our events are designed to make it easy to meet people without the awkward "just walked into a party where I don't know anyone" feeling. Hosts will introduce you around, and you'll be surprised how quickly conversations get going.

How can I host or partner on an event?

We love working with local businesses and members who want to host. Reach out to theindysocialclub@gmail.com with your idea and we'll go from there. If you want to find a group to hang with around town, then creating an event is as easy as sending an email!

Where can I follow along between events?

The best way to stay in the loop check the events calendar regularly. New events get posted throughout the month.

Still have questions?

We'd love to hear from you. Reach out anytime.
